Understanding Your Body Mass Index

If your BMI is below 18.5: Underweight - Consult a healthcare provider.

If your BMI is between 18.5 - 24.9: Normal - Healthy weight.

If your BMI is between 25 - 29.9: Overweight - Consider lifestyle changes.

If your BMI is above 30: Obese - Higher risk of health problems.
